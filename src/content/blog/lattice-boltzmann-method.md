It is an alternate way to simulate fluid flows. 
The partial differential equation that is solved is the transport equation of a probability density function.
Conventionally, the Navier Stokes Equation has been the basis of all major CFD applications.
In the Lattice Boltzmann method, the macroscopic physical quantities are obtained from mesoscopic distribution functions.
