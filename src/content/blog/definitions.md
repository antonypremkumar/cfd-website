## Reaction front

It is the leading edge of the reaction zone.

## Finite-rate Chemistry

In combustion literature, information regarding the important set of reactions and their reaction rates are gathered in a so-called chemical kinetic mechanism, hence the term finite-rate chemistry highlighting the finite set of intermediate elementary reactions of a chain reaction process.
